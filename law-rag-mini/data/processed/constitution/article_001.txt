Legal Text:
(1) India, that is Bharat, shall be a Union of States.
(2) The States and the territories thereof shall be as specified in the First Schedule.
(3) The territory of India shall comprise:
(a) the territories of the States,
(b) the Union territories,
(c) such other territories as may be acquired.

Explanation:
Article 1 defines India as a Union of States and describes its territorial composition. It clarifies that the First Schedule lists all States and Union Territories, and allows India to acquire new territory in the future. This establishes the territorial and federal structure of the country.
