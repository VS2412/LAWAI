Legal Text:
A person who after 1 March 1947 migrated from India to Pakistan shall not be deemed a citizen of India.
Proviso:
A person who returned to India under a permit for resettlement or permanent return shall be deemed to have migrated to India after 19 July 1948 for the purposes of Article 6.

Explanation:
Article 7 excludes those who migrated to Pakistan but restores citizenship to those returning with official resettlement permits.
