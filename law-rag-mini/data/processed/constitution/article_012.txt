Article 12 — Definition of the State

Legal Text:
In this Part, unless the context otherwise requires, “the State" includes:
- the Government and Parliament of India,
- the Government and Legislature of each State,
- all local or other authorities within the territory of India,
- all authorities under the control of the Government of India.

Explanation:
Article 12 defines “State” for the purpose of Fundamental Rights, ensuring that municipalities, government bodies, and public authorities are covered.
