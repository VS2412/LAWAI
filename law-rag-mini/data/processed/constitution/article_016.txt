Article 16 â€” Equality of Opportunity in Public Employment

Legal Text:
(1) There shall be equality of opportunity for all citizens in matters of State employment.

(2) No citizen shall be discriminated against in employment on grounds of religion, race, caste, sex, descent, place of birth, residence, or any of these.

(3) Parliament may prescribe residence requirements for certain State or Union Territory jobs.

(4) The State may provide reservations in public employment for backward classes not adequately represented.

(4A) The State may provide reservation in promotions, with consequential seniority, for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes not adequately represented.

(4B) The State may treat unfilled reserved vacancies as a separate class to be filled in subsequent years without affecting the 50% reservation ceiling.

Explanation:
Article 16 ensures equal opportunity in government jobs but permits reservation policies for social justice and representation.
